FAQ

How to start using your service?

All you need to do is contact our support team and get access to your personal account where you can find out all the advantages of our service.

Do you charge per minute or per second?

Second by second, starting from the first second. There is no charge to start a conversation.

How to call for free?

Contact support and we will provide you with access to test our service.

What are the benefits?

Free calls within the network. The cheapest calls to phones in any country, about 2-3 times, and sometimes more.

Is connection/registration free?

Yes.

Do you have a monthly (subscription) fee for outgoing calls?

No. Payment is made only for calls.

In which countries do your services operate?

Anywhere in the world where there is Internet.

How to top up your account?

The balance is replenished automatically, usually the money is credited to the account instantly. The balance can be topped up in your personal account by clicking “Top up account” at the top of the screen. You can access your personal account through our support service.
